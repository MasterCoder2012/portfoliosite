New Hostile Mounts!

The Zombie Horse

The Zombie Horse is now loose in Survival mode — and it’s absolutely not messing around.
This terrifying new unit spawns already mounted by a spear-wielding zombie, instantly turning it into a fast, hostile roaming threat you cannot ignore.
The rider is ultra-aggressive, constantly hunting players and unleashing a brutal dash attack that deals MASSIVE damage on impact.
If that dash lands, expect your health bar to evaporate.
Pair that with the Zombie Horse’s insane speed and mobility, and you’ve got an enemy that can close gaps in seconds, break formations, and delete anyone caught slipping.
It excels at ambushes, chasing fleeing players, and punishing bad positioning.

The Zombie Nautilus

The Drowned have a brand-new way to make waves!
Now wielding tridents, these fearsome mobs ride the Zombie Nautilus as they glide silently through oceans, patrolling the deep waters with deadly intent.
This combination of rider and mount makes them faster, more unpredictable, and far more dangerous than ever before.
Their patrols are relentless, and they can ambush unsuspecting players who wander too close to their watery domain.
Defeat the drowned rider to break their control over the Nautilus — but be warned, it won’t go down easily.
Once freed, you can claim the Nautilus for yourself, adding a powerful new mount to your arsenal and dominating the seas.
Mastering the Zombie Nautilus opens up tactical advantages, allowing you to traverse the oceans swiftly while keeping enemies on edge.
Underestimate this duo, and you’ll quickly find yourself outmatched in the deep.

The Camel Husk

Riding into the deserts atop towering camels, these undead warriors now roam the sun-scorched dunes with unstoppable determination.
The husk rider is relentless, using speed and aggression to strike anyone foolish enough to cross their path.
Their Parched companion adds an extra layer of danger, coordinating attacks and making every encounter unpredictable and deadly.
Together, they dominate the desert terrain, chasing down players and punishing poor positioning with devastating precision.
Defeat the husk rider and their Parched mount to break their control — but be ready for a tough fight, as they won’t give up easily.
Once the camel is freed, you can claim it as your own, gaining a swift and powerful desert mount to traverse the sands with ease.
Mastering this duo gives you a tactical edge, allowing you to patrol, ambush, or escape across the dunes while keeping foes on high alert.
Underestimating the husk rider and Parched companion can quickly turn the desert from a playground into a nightmare.
